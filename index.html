About Us

L'Esperienza Fluida was created to make exploring Italy feel light, simple and enjoyable. We focus on the essentials⁠; the food, the places, and the culture;⁠ then present them in a clear way so anyone can understand and appreciate them. Our goal is to bring the warmth of Italy to your screen without overwhelming you. Every page is designed to be calm, modern and welcoming, just like the experience of walking through an Italian street with no rush.
