Italian Food

A simple look at the flavors of Italy.

Northern Italy

Risotto – Creamy rice cooked slowly with broth.

Polenta – Soft or grilled cornmeal.

Speck – Lightly smoked Alpine ham.

Central Italy

Fresh Egg Pasta – Tagliatelle, pappardelle, lasagna.

Pecorino Cheese – Salty sheep milk cheese.

Bistecca Fiorentina – Tuscan-style steak.

Southern Italy

Pizza – Especially the Neapolitan classic.

Seafood – Octopus, sardines, calamari.

Citrus Fruits – Lemons from the Amalfi Coast.
