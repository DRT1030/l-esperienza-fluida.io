Italian Food

A simple look at the flavors of Italy.

Northern Italy

• Risotto – Creamy rice dishes cooked with broth.

• Polenta – Cornmeal served soft or grilled.

• Speck – Smoked ham from the Alps.

Central Italy

• Fresh Egg Pasta – Tagliatelle, pappardelle, lasagna.

• Pecorino Cheese – A salty sheep cheese.

• Bistecca Fiorentina – Famous Tuscan steak.

Southern Italy

• Pizza – Especially the Neapolitan style.

• Seafood – Octopus, sardines, calamari.

• Citrus Fruits – Lemons from the Amalfi Coast.
